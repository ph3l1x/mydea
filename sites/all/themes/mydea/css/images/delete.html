Account Development

The core of our business is account development for a solution sale. We create the desire, understand prospects’ processes and operations at a detailed level, and develop the urgency. As a result, we provide a qualified prospect to sales/partners so they can stay focused on what they do best- close business.
  • Validate/update/scrub current company list ensuring companies/titles match prospect profile
  • Develop new data driven prospecting lists
  • Manage strategic accounts – nurture your top identified prospects
  • Develop qualification metrics and understand product/solution/market
  • Find the correct decision maker and make contact by phone, email or other marketing tactics
  • Establish relationships to understand the prospect’s needs and pain points. Identify specific processes and where your services can provide business value
  • Create success measurement tracking/reporting on all activity
  • Seamlessly transfer the relationship with the prospect to your sales team - we appear as an extension of your sales department
  • Nurture accounts/contacts that due to a variety of reasons aren’t ready to engage
  • Follow up to ensure no opportunities are dropped
  • Transparently report in CRM
  • Create content for mass email/drip campaigns
