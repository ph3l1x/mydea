Dustin Bevins

Dustin Bevins graduated Boise State University with a degree in Business and Communications after growing up in rural Idaho. Dustin had the opportunity to influence many different business processes in his career at a top grocer company. With involvement in multiple aspects of purchasing, warehouse and process optimization continuing into a warehouse management role. Dustin was a crucial part of the team that investigated new technologies and choosing to implement voice in their warehouses. As such he was involved in ensuring the new technologies achieved optimization goals as well as boosting existing employee morale. He continued to support implementation processes and post implementation training and resource management. After leaving his career in grocery he chose to join the growing MyDea Marketing team taking on a crucial role as an Account Manager for multiple customers across several industry verticals. He is a leader in implementing new and insightful processes to ensure our teams continued success. Dustin’s unrelenting work ethic and inability to be defeated continues to set the tone for all those who work with and around him. Dustin leverages his experiences in multiple roles to ensure that we continue to be a leader in all segments of business.

Account Development

The core of our business is account development for a solution sale. We create the desire, understand prospects’ processes and operations at a detailed level, and develop the urgency. As a result, we provide a qualified prospect to sales/partners so they can stay focused on what they do best- close business.
  • Validate/update/scrub current company list ensuring companies/titles match prospect profile
  • Develop new data driven prospecting lists
  • Manage strategic accounts – nurture your top identified prospects
  • Develop qualification metrics and understand product/solution/market
  • Find the correct decision maker and make contact by phone, email or other marketing tactics
  • Establish relationships to understand the prospect’s needs and pain points. Identify specific processes and where your services can provide business value
  • Create success measurement tracking/reporting on all activity
  • Seamlessly transfer the relationship with the prospect to your sales team - we appear as an extension of your sales department
  • Nurture accounts/contacts that due to a variety of reasons aren’t ready to engage
  • Follow up to ensure no opportunities are dropped
  • Transparently report in CRM
  • Create content for mass email/drip campaigns
